## ✦ Who Am I?

I'm a **full-stack developer** obsessed with the intersection of  
**engineering and design** — where systems are scalable,  
interfaces are beautiful, and users *actually* enjoy what they use.

I build things that are:
- 🎨 **Pixel-precise** — design isn't decoration, it's function
- ⚡ **Blazing fast** — performance is a feature
- 📱 **Cross-platform** — web and native Android, both done right
- 🧠 **AI-integrated** — modern products deserve modern intelligence

> *"Any fool can write code that a computer can understand.*  
> *Good programmers write code that humans can understand."*  
> — Martin Fowler

### ✦ Sprintify — AI-Powered Project Management SaaS

</div>

> *Where teams stop managing chaos and start shipping greatness.*

**Sprintify** is a full-featured AI-powered SaaS platform that reimagines how modern teams plan, collaborate, and execute. Built for speed, clarity, and scale — it brings intelligent automation to your workflow without getting in the way.

**What makes it stand out:**
- 🤖 AI-driven task prioritization and sprint planning suggestions
- 📊 Real-time dashboards with live team activity feeds
- 🔄 Kanban + Scrum hybrid boards with drag-and-drop fluidity
- 🔔 Smart notifications and deadline tracking
- 🔐 Role-based access control for enterprise-ready teams

### ✦ Billiant — Invoicing & Client Management Platform

</div>

> *Professional billing, without the professional headache.*

**Billiant** is a sleek, intuitive platform that gives freelancers and small businesses full control over their client relationships and finances. Say goodbye to spreadsheet chaos — Billiant makes invoicing feel effortless.

**What makes it stand out:**
- 📄 One-click invoice generation with custom branding
- 👥 Complete client CRM — contacts, history, notes, all in one place
- 💰 Payment tracking with overdue alerts and status management
- 📈 Revenue analytics with visual breakdowns by client and period
- 🎨 Clean, distraction-free UI built for speed
